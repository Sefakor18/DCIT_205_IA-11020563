MPhil in Computer Science

The MPhil in Computer Science is a research-based program that provides students with advanced knowledge in Computer Science, including artificial intelligence, data science, Cybersecurity and more.Engaged in research projects, students are aided by renowned faculty to contribute in the creation of new knowledge. Our MPhil program prepares students for leadership roles in shaping the future of technology.

Duration: 2 years

MSc. in Artificial Intelligence

Explore the exciting field of artificial intelligence with our MSc. program. Learn about machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision and advanced algorithms that enables a student to develop important and innovative solutions for real-world problems.

Duration: 18 months
